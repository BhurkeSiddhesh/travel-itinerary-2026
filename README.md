# 🌿 Coorg · Mysuru · Ooty — May 2026

A premium, mobile-responsive travel itinerary for an 8-day self-drive circuit through the Western Ghats.

## ✨ Features
- **Mobile-First Design:** Fully optimized for modern iPhones (XR and newer) with a sleek hamburger menu and notch/safe-area support.
- **Interactive Single-Page App (SPA):** Seamless navigation between Overview, Pre-Trip Checklist, and Day-by-Day breakdowns.
- **Rich Aesthetics:** Glassmorphism UI, high-quality local imagery, and elegant typography (Cormorant Garamond & Jost).
- **Practical Logistics:** Detailed hour-by-hour timelines, logistics strips, stay cards, and critical "Flag Alerts" for peak season travel.
- **Offline Ready:** Built with vanilla HTML/CSS/JS for fast loading and easy printing support.

## 🗺️ The Circuit
1. **Kannur → Coorg:** Arrival and coastal-to-highland drive.
2. **Coorg Local:** Waterfalls, Namdroling Monastery, and coffee estate walks.
3. **Coorg Deep:** Dubare Elephant Camp and the source of the Kaveri.
4. **Coorg → Mysuru:** Heritage stays and royal palace visits.
5. **Mysuru Full Day:** Chamundi Hills, Zoo, and Brindavan Musical Gardens.
6. **Mysuru → Ooty:** Mudumalai wildlife drive and the 36-hairpin mountain ascent.
7. **Ooty & Coonoor:** Tea factories, toy trains, and Nilgiri viewpoints.
8. **Coimbatore → Home:** Final descent and flight from CJB.
